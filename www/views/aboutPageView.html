Confero was built by Reid Holmes and Rylan Cottrell to make it easier for conference organizers to provide high quality mobile programs for academic conferences. Confero is actively maintained and can be easily extended to support additional conferences. Special thanks to Jason Weathersby and the Mozilla for their support with FireFox OS, and Laura Inozemtseva and Oleksii Kononenko their suggestions and feedback. Confero was inspired by the Windows Phone My Conference app developed by Chris Bird and Thomas Zimmermann.

If you have any suggestions or encounter any problems, please let us know so we can continue to improve the app.

Confero can be accessed on the web at http://conferoapp.com, but is best experienced through mobile apps. These can be found for free at the following places:
FireFox OS Marketplace
Apple App Store
Google Play
BlackBerry World
